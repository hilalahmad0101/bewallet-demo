BetFast wallet Data Deletion Agreement
  1. Data Deletion Request:

    The User has the right to request the deletion of all personal data associated with their BetFast wallet account.

  2. Scope of Deletion:

    Upon receiving a valid deletion request, BetFast wallet will make reasonable efforts to delete all personal information provided by the User, including but not limited to account details, phone number, bank details, email address, password, transaction history, transfer history, and deposit history stored within the BetFast wallet application.

  3. Timeframe:

    BetFast wallet will process the data deletion request in a reasonable timeframe, ensuring that all relevant data is removed within a user-selected number of days after verification of the user request.

  4. Irreversible Deletion:

    The User understands that once the data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. BetFast wallet will take appropriate measures to ensure the irreversible nature of the deletion process.

  5. Exceptions:

    Certain data may be retained by BetFast wallet for legal or legitimate business purposes, such as records required by law or to resolve disputes. However, such retained data will not be used for any other purpose.

  6. Communication:

    BetFast wallet may contact the User to confirm the data deletion request or seek clarification if needed. The User agrees to respond promptly to such communications to facilitate the deletion process.

  7. Revocation:

    The User may revoke their data deletion request at any time before the completion of the deletion process. Revocation should be communicated to BetFast wallet in writing.

  8. Privacy Policy:

    This Agreement is subject to the terms and conditions of BetFast wallet's Privacy Policy, available on the BetFast wallet website.

By submitting a data deletion request, the User acknowledges that they have read and understood the terms of this Agreement and agrees to abide by them.
